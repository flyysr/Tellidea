The Life

Too many unhappy things make us learn to complain ,
but forget to make the change to live a better life!
One day, I walk and walk along with the sea side,
Calm down,
and still face the life with a smile face,
study hard,
go for the goal---to be a better man,
little by little,
block by block,
day by day,
then I find the life is not the way I always think,
instead, it is so sweet that I really has fallen in love with.
